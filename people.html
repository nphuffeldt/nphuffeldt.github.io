Nicholas Per Huffeldt (P.I.)

He is a Senior Scientist at the Greenland Institute of Natural Resources and a Visiting Research Fellow in the Migration Group at Lund University, SE. Before his current position he was a MSCA Individual Fellow at Lund Unversity; the Crown Prince Frederik Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellow at the Greenland Institute of Natural Resources with the University of Groningen, NL; and a Postdocoral Researcher at Aarhus University, DK. He recieved his Ph.D. from Wake Forest University, US and his M.Sc. from the University of Copenhagen, DK.
